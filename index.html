Teenage mental health

Many things can negatively affect teens' mental health. According to the website healthychildren.org, three main things that have large effects are academic pressure, tough schedules, and generally difficult day-to-day problems.

Academic pressure: the overwhelming pressure to get good grades, pass every test, do every assignment well, impress colleges, and get academic validation.

Tough schedules: long days, few breaks, many responsibilities, extracurriculars, personal concerns, and lack of sleep.

Difficult day-to-day problems: family problems, friend problems, romantic problems, bullying, loneliness, depression, anxiety, and just life being really difficult.
